Q: How do you determine which data to cache?

A: Use a variety of metrics to determine which data to cache, such as the popularity of the data, the frequency of access, and the size of the data.

A: Use a cache invalidation mechanism to remove stale data from the cache.

Rate Limiting patternâ€‹

Prevent news feed system from being overwhelmed by too many requests.

Toolsâ€‹

Nginx Apache Traffic Server Amazon API Gateway Google Cloud Rate Limit Service

Interview Questionsâ€‹

Q: How do you determine the appropriate rate limit? A: Use a variety of metrics to determine the appropriate rate limit, such as the number of requests per second, the number of requests per user, and the size of the requests. Q: How do you handle requests that exceed the rate limit? A: Use a variety of techniques to handle requests that exceed the rate limit, such as dropping the requests, queuing the requests, or returning an error message. Q: How do you ensure that the rate limiter is fair and does not discriminate against certain users or clients?
